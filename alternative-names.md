## Top Recommendations

**If pursuing IETF standardisation:**
- `agentpolicy.txt` — cleanest framing, maps naturally to "policy" language used in IETF drafts
- `aipermit.txt` — memorable, permission-forward, no prior art anywhere

**If prioritising broad web adoption:**
- `aimanifest.txt` — "manifest" already understood by developers (web app manifests, package manifests)
- `agentaccess.txt` — direct parallel to access control, intuitive

**If emphasising the consent/training angle:**
- `aiconsent.txt` — strong resonance with EU AI Act / GDPR framing
- `agentlicense.txt` — covers both action permissions and training consent
